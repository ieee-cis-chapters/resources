According to the rules of the 2012 T-shirt competition organized by the IEEE CIS, all participants have agreed that their designs will be provided under the Creative Commons License (cc by 3.0), which allows sharing and remix.

This means that the anyone is free to extend these designs, improve them. Also this means that that the designs will be available for our student members to use in promoting CIS to other students, by printing T-shirts, posters etc.

For more information visit: http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/
